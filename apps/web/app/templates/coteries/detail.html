Coterie Rules Reference
Formation
  • Coteries may have up to {{ coterie_max }} members (including the founder).
  • Each member must be an active character not already in another coterie.
  • Starting dots: distribute 5 dots across Chasse, Lien, and Portillon at creation.
  • Coteries formed after a timeskip receive starting dots assigned by staff.
Ratings
  • Chasse — Hunting grounds. Sets site entitlement and modifies hunting DCs for coterie channel hunts.
  • Lien — Social standing and boons owed to the coterie in the city.
  • Portillon — Haven security. Cannot exceed Chasse.

Coterie Spends
  • Cost is 3 XP per dot per member for Chasse, Lien, and Portillon.
  • All members must commit their XP share before the spend can be approved.
  • Once all members commit the spend moves to Funded and awaits staff approval.
  • Staff approves or denies — approval deducts XP from all members simultaneously.
