Coterie Rules Reference
Formation
  • Up to 6 members per coterie (including founder).
  • Each member must be an active character not already in another coterie.
  • At creation: distribute 5 dots across Chasse, Lien, and Portillon.
  • Coteries formed after a timeskip receive starting dots assigned by staff.
Ratings
  • Chasse — Hunting grounds. Determines site entitlement and coterie-channel hunting DCs.
  • Lien — Social standing and boons owed to the coterie.
  • Portillon — Haven security. Cannot exceed Chasse.
