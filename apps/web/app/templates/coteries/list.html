Coterie Rules Reference
Formation
  • Requires at least 3 founding members; maximum {{ coterie_max }} members total.
  • Each member must be an active character not already in another coterie.
  • At creation: distribute 5 dots across Chasse, Lien, and Portillon.
  • Each founding member contributes 2 free creation dots for coterie merits/advantages (all placement must be narratively justified).
  • Coterie Flaws may be taken at creation (unanimous consent) for additional creation dots.
  • Inactive players (2+ months) are removed along with any contributed backgrounds.
Ratings
  • Chasse — Hunting grounds. Determines site entitlement and coterie-channel hunting DCs.
  • Lien — Social standing and boons owed to the coterie.
  • Portillon — Haven security. Cannot exceed Chasse.

Coterie Spends (Ratings)
  • Cost is 3 XP per dot per member for Chasse, Lien, and Portillon.
  • All members must commit their share before staff can approve.
Merits & Advantages
  • Free creation dots + donated backgrounds pool together; maximum 3 dots per background from this pool.
  • Donated: any member may contribute one of their personal backgrounds at any rating; cannot stack the creation/donated pool above 3 dots.
  • Purchased: individual members buy coterie advantages post-creation at 3 XP per dot; no hard dot cap; requires staff approval.
